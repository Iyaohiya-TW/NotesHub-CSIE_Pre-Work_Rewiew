# Tier 4 (Project Evaluation)

Many interviewers spend more time on projects than coursework. They may ask:

-   Explain your architecture.
-   Why choose this framework?
-   Biggest challenge?
-   What would you improve?
-   Team collaboration?
-   Bug you spent the longest fixing?
-   How did you debug it?
-   What did you learn?
-   If starting over, what would you change?

These questions often reveal more about your practical ability than textbook knowledge.

***

# Tier 5 (Engineering Thinking)

Strong interviewers also assess how you think, not just what you know.

Examples:

-   How do you approach an unfamiliar technology?
-   How do you break down a complex problem?
-   How do you verify that your solution is correct?
-   How do you estimate the impact of a change?
-   How do you communicate technical issues to teammates?

For entry-level roles, clear reasoning and a structured approach can be as important as knowing the "right" answer.
